LawBoard Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

LawBoard respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. LawBoard may collect account information, case information, client information, reminders, legal document data, team collaboration data, finance records, settings, and technical information necessary to provide app functionality.

Data is stored using Firebase and related Google services. LawBoard does not sell user data and does not use case or client data for advertising purposes.

Users may request account deletion directly within the app: Menu → Profile → Account & Privacy → Delete Account, or by emailing isit.tech@yahoo.com.

For the complete Privacy Policy, contact: isit.tech@yahoo.com
